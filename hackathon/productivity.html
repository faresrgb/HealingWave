Tips for Improving Productivity

Try incorporating some of these productivity tips into your daily schedule. Monitor what does and doesn’t work for you so you can build a lasting plan to improve your productivity.

  • Stop Multitasking: It’s often tempting to try and multitask, but it's more effective to focus on one task at a time.
  • Set Small Goals: Break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps to create momentum.
  • Take a Break: Regular breaks help decrease stress and increase productivity. Try taking short breaks throughout the day.
  • The Five-Minute Rule: Commit to just five minutes on a task, often leading to extended productivity.
  • Time Blocking: Use time blocks to dedicate specific periods for each task, improving focus and efficiency.
